About Silver Oxy

Founded in 2016, Silver Oxy began with a simple mission: to provide pure and refreshing water that you can trust. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted brand, recognized for our commitment to quality and innovation. Our journey has been one of constant evolution. From our first bottle design in 2016 to our latest sleek and modern design in 2024, we’ve always prioritized both functionality and aesthetics. Our new label and bottle design reflect the growth of our brand and our dedication to meeting the highest standards of quality. At Silver Oxy, we provide clean and refreshing drinking water in 1L and 500ml sizes. Our eco-friendly packaging ensures a healthier planet while keeping you hydrated. The slogan, "Drink Silver, Feel The Oxy," perfectly encapsulates our promise to deliver purity and trust in every drop. This journey wouldn’t have been possible without the relentless efforts of Saleem Ansari, who worked tirelessly from scratch to lay the foundation of this brand, and Shehbaaz Ansari, who has passionately managed and driven the brand forward. Today, Silver Oxy stands as a symbol of purity, trust, and dedication—a legacy built on hard work and innovation.
